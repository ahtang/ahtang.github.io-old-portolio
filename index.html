About Me

I'm a 4th year Cognitive Science student at the University of California, San Diego and I aspire to design delightful experiences that solve everyday problems.

User behaviors and insights guide my design process and inspire me to think creatively to help individuals reach their goals with ease. I believe that functionality is beautiful and enjoy making experiences more inviting by researching with a user-centered approach and creating engaging visuals.

In my spare time you can find me jamming to indie tunes alone in my car, adding a pint-sized scoop of ice cream to my desserts, and kicking major butt in Tetris but less butt in League of Legends.
